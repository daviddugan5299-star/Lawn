About GreenSpace

Dedicated to creating beautiful, healthy outdoor spaces for over a decade. Our expertise and passion for lawn care make us your trusted partner in landscape excellence.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, GreenSpace Lawn & Garden began as a small family business with a simple mission: to help homeowners create the outdoor spaces of their dreams. What started as a local lawn care service has grown into a comprehensive garden center serving thousands of satisfied customers.

Our founder, Michael Thompson, brought over 20 years of horticultural experience to the business. His passion for sustainable lawn care practices and commitment to customer education has shaped our company's values and approach to this day.

Today, we're proud to offer not just premium products, but also the expertise and support needed to achieve lasting success in lawn and garden maintenance. Every product we sell is tested by our team and backed by our knowledge.
